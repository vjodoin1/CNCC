Welcome to Central Nebraska Chess Club (CNCC)

Bringing together chess enthusiasts from all over Nebraska!

About Us

The Central Nebraska Chess Club (CNCC) is the heart of the chess community in Central Nebraska and beyond, based in Kearney we are welcoming players of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned competitor, a casual enthusiast, or just learning the game, CNCC provides a friendly and engaging environment to grow your chess skills.

What We Offer:

  • ♟ Weekly Club Meetings – Enjoy casual games, structured lessons, and friendly competition in a welcoming atmosphere.
  • ♟ Tournaments & Championships – Compete in our seasonal events, including the CNCC Championship, where players earn trophies and medals based on performance.
  • ♟ Lessons & Training – Improve your game with expert guidance, group classes, community.
  • ♟ Community & Outreach – We’re committed to growing chess in Central Nebraska through partnerships, school programs, and public events.

Join us to sharpen your skills, make new friends, and be part of a thriving chess community!
